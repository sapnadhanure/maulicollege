Characteristics of the Mauli College


The B.A. in Yogshastra is a comprehensive 3-year undergraduate program that blends classical yogic philosophy with modern practices. Students explore asanas, pranayama, meditation, yoga therapy, and ancient texts like Patanjali Yoga Sutras.


  • Magazines and periodicals like Yojana, Kurukshetra, Lokrajya are available for students.
  • National and state level newspapers are available for students to read.
  • Biomatric attendance of students.
  • College's own printed notes.
  • Notebook provided by the college for students to take notes
  • Dress Code for every student.
  • Study room available for students.
  • Professor available for Doubt Section in study room.
  • Students are present in college full time. (College timings are from 08.00 am to 06.30 pm).
  • Expert, knowledgeable and experienced faculty in the field of competitive examinations.
  • Scenic and educational environment
  • Well-equipped library. Various reference books available
  • E-classroom (INTERACTIVE PANNEL BOARD, PROJECTOR) available.
  • Updated and innovative educational materials available.
  • Transport facilities available for students. (TRANSPORT – BUS)
  • Hostel and food facilities available for students from outside the village (HOSTEL & MESS).
  • Various educational and social initiatives for the all-round development of students.
  • Grand sports complex. Various sports facilities for students.
  • Organizing trips through the college, visits to various historical places every year.

About College Structure

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Criteria: Minimum age 17 to 60.

  • 12th pass students from any branch and MCVC pass students

  • Medical Fitness: Student should be medically fit.

  • 12th pass students from any branch and MCVC pass students.

  • Students can choose one medium for the examination from Marathi, Sanskrit and English medium. However, teaching will be done in Marathi medium only. However, for Sanskrit subject and Sanskrit Certificate Examination, the medium will be Sanskrit only.
