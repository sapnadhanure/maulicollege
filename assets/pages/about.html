About Mauli College (Yogshastra)

Mauli (Yogshastra) College, established in 2024, is a prominent institution situated at Jalna, Maharashtra. The college is aims to integrate traditional Ayurvedic practices with modern medical advancements. Academic Programs

At MAULI College, we provide a holistic yoga education rooted in tradition yet enriched with modern methods. Our courses help students build discipline, strength, mindfulness, and inner peace.

Practicing yoga regularly promotes a healthy lifestyle. It improves flexibility, enhances breathing, boosts immunity, and reduces stress levels. Mental clarity and emotional balance are among the key benefits of a committed yoga practice.

Yoga Poses (Asanas) Provide to Our Faculty

Surya Namaskar

Surya Namaskar, also known as Sun Salutation, is a series of twelve linked yoga poses performed in a dynamic flow. It's a popular and beneficial practice, often performed in the morning facing the rising sun to harness its energy. The sequence involves a continuous movement between the poses, creating a full-body workout and promoting overall well-being.

Padmasana

Padmasana, also known as the Lotus Pose, is a seated yoga posture where each foot is placed on the opposite thigh, symbolizing purity and enlightenment. It's a foundational yoga pose, often used for meditation and known for promoting calmness and stability. The pose is widely practiced in Hindu, Buddhist, and Jain traditions.

Bhujangasana

Bhujangasana, also known as Cobra Pose, is a back-bending yoga asana that stretches the spine, chest, and abdomen while strengthening the back and arms. It's commonly practiced in Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutations) and offers numerous physical and mental benefits.

Vrikshasana

Vrikshasana, also known as Tree Pose, is a balancing yoga asana that involves standing on one leg with the other foot placed on the inner thigh or calf. It's named "Vrikshasana" because the pose resembles a tree. This asana offers numerous benefits, including improved balance, strength, flexibility, and mental clarity.

Shavasana

Shavasana, also known as Corpse Pose, is a resting yoga asana where you lie flat on your back with your body relaxed and limbs comfortably apart. It's typically the concluding pose in a yoga session and is used for deep relaxation, stress reduction, and mindfulness.

Vajrasan

Vajrasana, also known as the Thunderbolt Pose or Diamond Pose, is a kneeling asana in hatha yoga and modern yoga. It involves kneeling with the thighs perpendicular to the floor and sitting back on the heels. This pose is believed to improve digestion, strengthen the back, and calm the mind.
